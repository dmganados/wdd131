The Philippines

Data

  • Area: 300,000 square kilometers (115,831 square miles)
  • Population: Approximately 113 million (as of 2023)
  • Capital: Manila
  • Languages: Filipino (based on Tagalog) and English are the official languages. There are also numerous regional languages such as Cebuano, Ilocano, Hiligaynon, and others.
  • Currency: Philippine Peso (PHP)
  • Time Zone: Philippine Time (PHT), UTC +8
  • Calling Code: +63
  • Internet TLD: .ph
